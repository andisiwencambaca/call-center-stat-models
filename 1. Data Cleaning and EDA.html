RWFD: CALL CENTER OPERATIONS DATA¶

The purpose of this notebook is to investigate the call center operations dataset. I will be conducting several analyses to help me prepare to buidling multiple statistical models. My hope is that this EDA will provide me with enough information to know the distribution of the data, the correlation and variability between columns.
